Mandy Perea was born in Pomona, CA. They are currently an artist at CalArts working on a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree. Their studio work includes primarily ceramicware and insect taxidermy, however they are also interested in pursuing video game design. Throughout all mediums, they attempt to bridge the gap between personal interests and identity issues such as anxiety and presentation, isolation, and self-doubt. In their free time, Mandy enjoys playing video games (research), and collecting objects from nature.
